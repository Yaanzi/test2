FERTILIZATION

ZYGOTE (2n)

must contain the correct number of chromosome in order to produce a healthy individual.

- diploid

- contains two set of chromosomes(mother) (father)

MEIOSIS

- results in the production of daughter cells containing half number of chromosomes of the parent cells.

- There are four daughter cells produced after one meiotic process because the cell divides twice in meiosis.

- The daughter cells after meiosis are not alike because of the manner the chromosomes divide.

- type of cell division used by multicellular organism in the formation of reproductive cells (gametes)
